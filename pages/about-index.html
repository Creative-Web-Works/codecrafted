Welcome to Code Crafted!
We are an innovative web design and development agency based in Teesside, dedicated to crafting exceptional online experiences for businesses. Our passion lies in creating custom, high-quality static websites that leave a lasting impression. In today's competitive digital landscape, we understand the significance of a powerful online presence, and we are here to help your business thrive.

At Code Crafted, we believe that every website should be a work of art. Our team of seasoned professionals combines technical expertise with creative brilliance to deliver visually stunning, functional, responsive, and user-friendly websites. Whether you're a small business or a large corporation, we tailor our solutions to meet your unique needs and surpass your expectations.

We take pride in our streamlined and stress-free website development process, ensuring your journey with us is effortless. With our state-of-the-art Project Management Dashboard (PMD), you'll have full visibility into the development milestones of your website. Our PMD empowers you to collaborate with our team, share essential information, upload files, and stay updated on the progress of your website in real-time. Transparency and communication are at the heart of our approach, guaranteeing your satisfaction at every step.

Our team of experts meticulously handles every aspect of your website's creation, from conceptualization to hosting. During the design phase, we value your input and actively incorporate your feedback to ensure the end result aligns perfectly with your vision. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we won't rest until every detail of your website is flawlessly executed.
